Machilipatnam

Machilipatnam also called as Bandar is one of the town’s in Andhra Pradesh. It is the headquarters of Krishna District. In 1st century AD it was famous for a thin cotton cloth named muslin, hence named Masulipatnam. Click here for more details on the history of Machilipatnam.
